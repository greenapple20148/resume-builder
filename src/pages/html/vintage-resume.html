Personal Statement §
A seasoned editor and literary consultant with over fifteen years of distinguished service in book publishing, periodical editing, and manuscript development. Known for a meticulous eye, deep reverence for the written word, and an abiding commitment to elevating both emerging and established authors. Experienced in managing editorial teams, cultivating author relationships, and guiding manuscripts from conception through to critical and commercial success.
